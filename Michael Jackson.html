Michael Jackson

What he is known for:

During his four-decade career, his contributions to music, dance, and fashion, made him a global figure in popular culture. He influenced artists across many music genres. Though stage and video performances, he popularized complicated street dance moves such as the moonwalk, which he named, as well as the robot. He was awarded 834 awards in his career.

Honors and Awards:

 
  • 13 Grammy Awards
  • Grammy Legend Award
  • Grammy Lifetime Achievement Award
  • 6 Brit Awards
  • 5 Billboard Music Awards
  • 24 American Music Awards
  • 28 Guinness World Records
  • World's Most Successful Entertainer in 2006
  • Member of the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame
 
 Born: August 29, 1958 / Died: June 25, 2009
